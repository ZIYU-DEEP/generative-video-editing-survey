% ==================================== %
\begin{abstract}

The advancement of generative models has significantly transformed the field of video editing, offering superior capabilities while reducing computational demands. In this paper, we survey the adoption of various generative models in video editing. We detail strategies for building and refining these models, and highlight techniques for practical application. This work synthesizes key insights from extensive research and provides direction for practitioners and researchers. We present the necessary background on generative models, discuss their relationship with video editing, and demonstrate their real-world applicability. We propose a metric for generation efficiency, offering a basis for comparing different models. We conclude by outlining major open challenges and future directions in the field of generative video editing.
\end{abstract}
